## 📘 Project Overview
This project is developed as part of the Digital Literacy course. The aim of this project is to understand essential digital skills such as online safety, professional communication, use of digital tools, and awareness about cybercrime.

The project consists of five tasks, each focusing on a different aspect of digital literacy.

---

## 🖼️ Task 1 – Digital Literacy Infographic
In this task, I created a digital literacy awareness infographic using Canva. The infographic explains the concept of digital literacy and highlights its importance for students. It includes topics such as useful digital tools, safe internet practices, professional online presence, and email etiquette.

The design is visually appealing and easy to understand, using icons and simple text. This task helped me improve my creativity and ability to present information effectively.

📁 Folder: `task-1-presentation/`

---

## 💼 Task 2 – Digital Portfolio
In this task, I created and updated my professional profiles on platforms such as GitHub and LinkedIn. These platforms help in building a strong digital presence and showcasing skills to potential employers.

I created a GitHub profile README and updated my LinkedIn profile with my academic details. Screenshots of these profiles are included in the folder. This task helped me understand the importance of maintaining a professional online identity.

📁 Folder: `task-2-portfolio/`

---

## 💻 Task 3 – Platforms Exploration
In this task, I explored coding and collaboration platforms. I completed a beginner-level coding challenge on HackerRank, which helped me develop basic problem-solving skills.

Additionally, I created a Google Form consisting of five questions related to digital literacy awareness. This form can be used to test knowledge among students. This task enhanced my understanding of online tools used for learning and collaboration.

📁 Folder: `task-3-platforms/`

---

## 📧 Task 4 – Email Etiquette
In this task, I learned how to write professional emails. I created two email drafts: one for requesting an assignment extension and another for applying for a summer internship.

I also prepared a checklist of social media do’s and don’ts to promote responsible online behavior. This task helped me understand the importance of proper communication and maintaining professionalism in digital interactions.

📁 Folder: `task-4-email-etiquette/`

---

## 🛡️ Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness
In this task, I created a detailed case study on cybercrime (phishing/UPI fraud), explaining how such attacks happen, who is targeted, and what the consequences are.

I also prepared a prevention checklist with practical tips to stay safe online, including financial safety measures. This task increased my awareness of cyber threats and taught me how to protect personal and financial information.

📁 Folder: `task-5-cybercrime/`
